#### Week 16  - Make a contact with a xOTA station ‚úÖ

I've been 'doing' [SOTA](https://www.sota.org.uk/) and [WOTA](https://www.wota.org.uk/) for a long time now. Over a decade, so I have a few contacts to draw on.
